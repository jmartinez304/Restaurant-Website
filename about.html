Our story About Nonna's Table

Our story Since 2007

Since Giovanni was a young boy he dreamed of having his own restaurant. Inspired by his grandmother's cooking he wanted to make a great restaurant that captured those memories. Wanting to make that dream a reality, Giovanni has had a lifetime experience to bring it into fruition. From helping his nonna, to studying in university, and up until moving to the United States in 2005. Five years of work and savings had built the funds to open Nonna's Table. In Nonna's Table every bite has a sense of 'grandmother's cooking'. With time and care, Giovanni expanded the restaurant and is recognized in the community as a diamond in the rough that always takes you back to a little town in Italy.

Our vision Always fresh ingredients

Here at Nonna's Table our vision is to earn the respect of our patrons and provide a humble dining experience that can include the whole family. We wish to create an environment that will set us above local restaurants and present exceptional service, cleanliness and cultural value. While providing the freshest ingredients we will strive to make every patron smile with a belly full of rich, authentic italian food.

Our Restaurant Warm atmosphere

Nonna’s Table is an Italian cuisine restaurant located in Orlando, Florida. It is a casual restaurant that’s great for the family! The décor inside makes you feel like you are in Italy. With our carefully constructed menu we bring you the best flavors Italy has to offer at a price that can’t be beaten. While the menu is rooted in tradition, our chefs focus on elevating Italian ingredients and classic flavors to create modern classics. The pasta is made by hand fresh every day to bring you an authentic taste you won’t forget.
