Our story Love our food

Nonna's Table is the home and brainchild of Chef Giovanni Moretti. Since 2010, Nonna's Table has served central Florida citizens a little taste of Italy. Inspired by his grandmother's cooking he wanted to make a great restaurant that captured those memories. Patrons and restaurant goers praise Nonna's Table for the simplicity and welcoming atmosphere. Every bite has a sense of 'grandmother's cooking'.
